WeatherNow

Welcome to WeatherNow, your one-stop weather app. Get real-time weather, search cities worldwide, and see a 5-day forecast. Switch between Celsius and Fahrenheit.

  • Real-Time Updates: Stay prepared with instant weather info.
  • City Search: Check weather in cities worldwide.
  • 5-Day Forecast: Plan your week ahead.
  • Temp Conversion: Switch units effortlessly.

Tech Used:

  • HTML: Structure & layout.
  • CSS: Stylish, user-friendly design.
  • Bootstrap: Responsive & flexible.
  • JavaScript: Dynamic updates.
