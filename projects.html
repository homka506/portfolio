WeatherNow - Your Real-Time Weather Forecast App

Welcome to WeatherNow, your one-stop weather app. Get real-time weather, search cities worldwide, and see a 5-day forecast. Switch between Celsius and Fahrenheit.

Key project pages include:

  • Real-Time Weather Updates: WeatherNow provides you with up-to-the-minute weather information, ensuring you're always prepared for whatever Mother Nature has in store.
  • City Search: Easily search for cities around the globe to check their current weather conditions. Whether you're planning a trip or just curious about the weather elsewhere, WeatherNow has you covered.
  • 5-Day Forecast: Get a glimpse into the future with our 5-day weather forecast. Plan your week accordingly with a detailed outlook on upcoming weather patterns.
  • Temperature Conversion: WeatherNow allows you to switch between Celsius and Fahrenheit temperature formats effortlessly. Customize your experience to match your preferred units.

Stay informed and make the most of your day with WeatherNow. No matter where you are or where you're going, WeatherNow has the weather information you need at your fingertips. Download our app today and embrace the convenience of staying connected to the weather, anytime, anywhere.

Tech stack: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript.

Mecanicus

Mecanicus is a website for an auto repair shop that specializes in restoring and selling automobiles. The website was developed using the Webflow platform and incorporates HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and the Finsweet library to implement various functional features.

Key project pages include:

  • Acheter (Buy): Users can browse available automobiles for sale and access information about them, including photos, specifications, and prices.
  • Vendre (Sell): Information about how to offer one's car for sale through "Mecanicus."
  • House of Meca: Description of "Mecanicus" garage, its services, and features.
  • News: Page for publishing news, announcements, and updates related to the auto shop and the automotive industry.
  • Videos: Access to video materials related to the shop's work and automobile restoration.
  • Autopedia: A collection of useful articles and resources related to automotive technology and maintenance.
  • Recherche (Search): Ability to search among available cars based on various criteria (make, model, year, etc.).
  • Contact: Contact information for reaching "Mecanicus," including the address, phone number, and a contact form.
Additionally, data migration from the old website was carried out, preserving valuable data and resources for users."

Tech stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript,Finsweet library

Du Vert Dans La Vigne

"Du Vert Dans La Vigne" is an independent grassroots organization dedicated to the preservation and conservation of the environment and nature. The organization works on vineyard and winemaking projects aimed at supporting sustainable and environmentally friendly methods of grape cultivation and wine production. This project aims to create a landing page for "Du Vert Dans La Vigne," which will serve as an informational and communication hub for the organization and its activities. The landing page is being developed on the Webflow platform and utilizes technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create the website.

Tech stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript.
